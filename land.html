Land Rover

Land Rover is a British brand of predominantly four-wheel drive, off-road capable vehicles, owned by multinational car manufacturer Jaguar Land Rover, since 2008 a subsidiary of India's Tata Motors. JLR currently builds Land Rovers in Brazil, China, India, Slovakia, and the United Kingdom.
As one of the most iconic British car manufacturers in history, Land Rover is known worldwide for its incredible four-wheel-drive vehicles. Tracing its roots back to 1948 with the introduction of the Series I Land Rover, the brand quickly grew in popularity, especially in the off-road market.

In their 2019 reliability survey, Land Rover landed at the bottom of the pile of manufacturers they reviewed. Both brand new (0-3 years) and older Land Rover models (3-8 years) earned a low reliability rating, having been awarded just 1-star out of 5.
